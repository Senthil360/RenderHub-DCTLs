### 🌟 NEW: RH Subtractive Mixer
* **Subtractive Color Grading in CMY Density Space:** Converts the image into CMY Dye Density Space to simulate physical film dye absorption for rich, deep, and thick cinematic colors.
* **Luminance Lock:** Mathematically preserves original perceptual brightness while applying heavy subtractive saturation or color shifts.
* **Pure Hue Isolation:** Isolates color density from neutral gray density to prevent shifts from affecting white balance.
* **6 Subtractive Shift Sliders & Subtractive Saturation:** Organic hue manipulation across Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Red, Green, and Blue vectors without video-like brightness boosts.
